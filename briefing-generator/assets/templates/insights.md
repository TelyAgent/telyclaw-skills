# Insights Template

Focuses on extracting opinions, trends, and actionable takeaways. Best for decision-making.

## Output Format

```markdown
# {Channel names} · Insights Briefing
**Time range:** {start date} ~ {end date}

---

## Key Findings

1. **{Finding — a concise conclusion}**
   - Source: {channel name} ({N} related messages)
   - Details: {2-3 sentences on background and implications}

2. **{Finding}**
   ...

---

## Trend Signals
- {Emerging trends or directional shifts}
...

---

## Worth Watching
- {Upcoming events, signals to monitor}
...

---

Based on N messages across M channels
```

## Rules

- Key findings must be **conclusions**, not summaries — say what it means.
- Every finding must cite specific sources.
- Trend signals should highlight direction (rising/falling/pivoting/new).
- Avoid empty jargon; every insight must carry information.
